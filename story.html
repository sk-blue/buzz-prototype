Our Story

Happy Hive Studio was bloomed from a simple idea — a cosy jolly space for the most meaningful moments.

We noticed there are few cosy yet affordable spaces designed for gatherings or celebrations, where people come together full of energy, creativity, and life.

Every corner of the studio is designed to feel inviting, flexible, and joyful — so you can focus on what truly matters: the people and the memories you’re creating.

HappyHive Studio is made for all ages: adults, kids, or babies. Think birthday parties, milestone celebrations, creative workshops, baby showers, photoshoots, or any little events you could think of.

We’re here to help curate a space for you to celebrate, gather, create, host, or just be there.

Why choose us?

A young mum was struggling to find a bright and cosy space to host her baby boy’s 1st birthday party within budget. That very real problem became the seed for this idea.

The result is a venue concept that aims to be warm, bright, practical, photogenic, and gentler on the wallet than the usual rental circus.

Built for the little moments that matter.
