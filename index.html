What the space is for

The concept is simple: create a bright, cosy, reasonably priced venue for hosts who want a pleasant space without paying luxury-hotel nonsense money. It is especially suited to family moments, community gatherings, and small creative events.

Birthdays & family parties

A softer, more welcoming alternative to expensive event venues — especially for young families hosting baby birthdays, children’s celebrations, and small gatherings with relatives and friends.

Workshops & classes

Ideal for enrichment sessions, parent-child activities, private classes, and community-led events that need a practical, comfortable room with furniture and basic setup support.

Photo studio & brand pop-ups

The neutral palette and clean furnishings also make the space suitable for small product shoots, content creation, studio-style sessions, and local brand pop-up use.

What guests will find inside

The space isn’t just an empty room with emotional aspirations. It’s meant to be functional, host-friendly, and usable from day one.

Fixtures Pantry, utility room, washroom, changing room, curtains, TV corner, electrical points, kids play corner, and backdoor access for loading and unloading.
Furniture TV, TV console, coffee table, movable tables and chairs for adults and kids, water dispenser, diaper changing station, baby cot, and shelves.
Family-friendly additions Nintendo setup, carpet, playmat, ball pit, LEGO sets, board games, studio flashlight, and space that supports both children and adults.
Safety & comfort Washable non-slip floors, rounded furniture corners, child-safe layout, secured shelves, fire extinguishers, first aid kits, and emergency exit signage.
